About


I'm a full stack web developer, previously a microfabrication engineer working to create new technologies for the biomedical field. I thrive on problem solving and pursue intelligent, intuitive design. I love building applications and bringing useful solutions to life. I feel energized and excited learning the latest technologies. When I'm not working I'm playing board games, enjoying my family and teaching my new baby girl her first Scottish words. I originally moved from Glasgow in Scotland, I moved to Seattle in 2010 as a Research Engineer at the University of Washington. I left in 2013 and spent time living and working on the East Coast near DC and the Mid West not far from Chicago before coming back to Seattle in 2015.
